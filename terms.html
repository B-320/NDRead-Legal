What NDReady does

NDReady is a camera settings assistant for action camera riders. It reads light from a photo you take, calculates ND filter and exposure settings for your specific camera, and saves session notes on your device. All processing happens locally on your iPhone.

NDReady provides recommendations based on light metering and exposure math. It does not guarantee any particular outcome for your footage. Conditions, camera firmware, and rider technique all affect results.
